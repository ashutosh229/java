operators:
1. arithmetic:
- +, -, *, /, %

2. assignment:
- count op= value means "count = count op value" where op = {+,-,*,/,%}

3. unary:
- +, -, ++, --, ! 

4. comparison or relational:
- >,<,>=,<=,==,!=

5. logical:
- &&, ||, !

6. bitwise:
-&, |, ~ (logical bitwise)
-^ (XOR)
-<<, >>, (left and right shift) 
-<<<,>>> (unsigned letf and right shift)

7. ternary:
- ans = condition?value1:value2 

8. instance of:
-checks whether the object is the instance of a specific class or not
